I BELIEVE IN COMMERCE, THE FATHER ALMIGHTY, CREATOR OF DESIRES AND GOODS,

AND IN CAPITALISM, ITS ONLY SYSTEM, OUR PROSPERITY, WHICH WAS CONCEIVED BY THE GREED OF MAN,

BORN OF THE RELENTLESS PURSUIT OF PROFIT, SUSTAINED BY THE BLOOD OF THE MASSES.

HE DESCENDED INTO LEGAL OBSTACLES; ON THE THIRD DAY HE BOUGHT HIS WAY OUT.

HE ASCENDED INTO EXTREME WEALTH, AND IS SEATED AT THE RIGHT HAND OF COMMERCE, THE FATHER ALMIGHTY,

FROM THERE HE WILL COME TO JUDGE THE RICH AND THE POOR.

I BELIEVE IN CONSUMERISM, THE HOLY CHURCH OF COMMERCE, THE ENSLAVEMENT OF SOULS, THE SACRED BUYOSPHERE, THE MANIPULATION OF DESIRES, THE ETERNITY OF HUMAN GREED, AND DEBT EVERLASTING.

AMEN.
